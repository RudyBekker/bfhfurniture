    ABOUT BFH Furniture

    BFH Furniture is an contemporary home furnishing store headquartered in Western Cape. Founded by Bradley who dreamed of creating beautiful homes in their country, the goal of
    BFH Furniture is to enable everyone to create the home they envision without breaking the bank.
    Whether you’re looking to redecorate a room or renovate an entire house, we’re here to help you find the furniture
    and accessories that will make that dream a reality. BFH Furniture also has monthly giveaways to boost your chances of winning. 

    Factory Prices

    We bring you The Best Prices in South Africa on All Beds, Headboards & Home Accessories for you to make your House a Home again, without breaking the bank.

    Locally Manufactured

    We partner with the Top Manufacturers in South Africa to handcraft each of our products to ensure you get the Best Quality at Affordable Prices.
